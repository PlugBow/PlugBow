# GriefPrevention Plugin Documentation

## Commands
- **/claim**: Claims a piece of land for your own.
- **/abandonclaim**: Abandons the claim you are standing in.
- **/trust player**: Trusts a player in your claim.

## Permissions
- `griefprevention.claim`: Allows the player to use the /claim command.
- `griefprevention.trust`: Allows the player to trust other players in their claim.

## Examples
- **Claiming a land**: Using the /claim command in the area you want to claim.
- **Trusting a player**: Execute `/trust Steve` to allow Steve access.
